Goal:

The Goal of Chess is to either Trap or Capture the opposing players king

Number of Players: 2

Pieces:

6
Piece Picture Movement
Pawn

First Move: Forward 1 or 2
Other Move: Forward 1
Attack:Diagonally left or right 1.
Bishop Unlimited Movement Diagonally Left or Right, Forward or Back.
Attacks:Directly
Rook

Unlimted movement Left, Right, Forward or Back
Attacks: Directly
Knight

Movement in an L type fashion
Forward 2, left or right 1
Back 2, left or right 1
left 2, forward or back 1
right 2, forward or back 1
Attacks:directly
Queen Unlimited movement left, right, forward, back, and diagonally
Attacks:Directly
King Limited Movement left,right,forward,back, and diagonally
limit 1 space
Attacks directly
Killing or trapping opposing King means victory
